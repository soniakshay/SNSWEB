Education & Awarness

Sarus Nature Society organize various activities for Conservation of Environment and Wildlife ecosystem like Bird watching programmes, Drawing compititions, Elocution competition, Environment Quiz, Bicycle Rally, Wildlife Photo Exhibitions, Wildlife Film Screening and Shibirs. Awarness campaigns is also conducted in various villages to sensitize rural people about cultural, ecological and environmental significance.
