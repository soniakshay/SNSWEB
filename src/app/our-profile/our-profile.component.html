Our Profile

Sarus Nature Society(SNS), founded in 1997, is a Wildlife & Nature Conservation NGO based in Dholka, Dist. Ahmedabad (Gujarat), India. Our focus is to conserve Ecology & Environment, Wildlife, Natural resources for an improved environment. Its efforts for undertaking Environment Education and Conservation Activities especially for Conservation of Sarus Crane which is an endangered species listed in Red Data list of IUCN, Vulnerable species and under the Schedual-IV of the Wildlife (Protection) Act 1972.

Conservation is a constant process which must adjust to Social, Economic, Environmental and Scientific advancements. We anticipate that without mandatory participation of human being no amount of effort can sustain any threatened Ecosystem, Natural resources and Biodiversity.

Recognizing the fact that this species found mainly in paddy fields and wetlands. We took the people of respective villages into confidence and convinced them about the need of conservation of the species. Not only that but we motivated them to the extent that now this villagers are protecting the species at their own and are also providing vital information about the life cycle of Sarus Crane.

We also identified various threats faced by this endangered species and contacted various authorities personally to overcome these problem and reducing the threats. Sarus Nature Society (SNS) also took various steps for rescue and treatment of the injured birds, Education and Awarness and Conservation programmes.
