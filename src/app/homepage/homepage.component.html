Sarus Nature Society

Sarus Nature Society(SNS), founded in 1997, is a Wildlife & Nature Conservation NGO based in Dholka, Dist. Ahmedabad (Gujarat), India. Our focus is to conserve
Ecology &
Environment, Wildlife, Natural resources for an improved environment. Its efforts for undertaking Environment Education and Conservation Activities especially for Conservation of Sarus Crane which is an endangered species listed in Red Data list of IUCN, Vulnerable species and under the Schedual-IV of the Wildlif (Protection) Act 1972.

Conservation is a constant process which must adjust to Social, Economic, Environmental and Scientific advancements. We anticipate that without mandatory participation of human being no amount of effort can sustain any threatened Ecosystem, Natural resources and Biodiversity.
